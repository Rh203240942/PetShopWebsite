Details about Pet House

We’re a friendly local pet shop dedicated to helping you care for the animals you love.

From food and treats to toys and everyday essentials, we provide quality products for pets of all kinds.

Our team loves animals and is always happy to offer advice or help you find what you need.

We aim to create a warm, welcoming place where pets and their owners feel at home.
